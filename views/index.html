Where did the Gestrichs come from?

While Gestrichs identified themselves as German, immigration records indicate they were living in Wysoka which is now part of Poland. This is not surprising as nearly 40% of inhabitants of Wysoka were Germans who were colonizing the region at the time. Germans living in Wysoka Poland maintained their own distinct culture -- German-speaking and practicing Lutherans -- as did the Gestrichs.

Who were the first Gestrichs in the U.S. ?

Below are German immigration records. The top record is of Ottilie Gestrich who departed with her infant son Hugo for the United States on 8/30/1871 (my great-great-great grandomother). Her husband Heinrich Gestrich departed a few months earlier on 4/19/1871. His occupation upon arrival was a baker. They were both 25 years of age at the time. These are the oldest records I have of our ancestry in the Gestrich line. The records both list Ottilie and Heinrich's hometown as Wissek (or Wysoka)

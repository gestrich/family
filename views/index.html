Where did the Gestrichs come from?

While the first Gestrichs certainly identified themselves as German, immigration records indicate they were living in Wysoka which is now part of Poland. This is not surprising as nearly 40% of inhabitants of Wysoka were Germans who were colonizing the region at the time. Germans living in Wysoka Poland maintained their own distinct culture -- German-speaking and practicing Lutherans -- as did the Gestrichs.
